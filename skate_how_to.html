How To Kickflip

Stop! Important Checklist!

  • Get A Skateboard
  • Get Skate Shoes
  • Younger the 13? recommended safety wear
  • Comfy Pants
  • Water is needed skating is a serious exercise
  • Be patient and willing to fix your mistakes
  • Have Fun!

Steps : Kickflip

  1. Get used to the feel of your board
  2. Start off on a soft surface like carpet (recommended for women)
  3. Bend down a bit then throw your upper body into the air while doing the following 3 steps:
  4. Place Foot on the tail of the board, then push down in a quick, snapping fashion
  5. Then at the same time drag your front foot up and off the side of the board
  6. Then bring your knees as close to your face as possible
  7. Then land with steez, and skate off like it was nothing

If you dont get it the first few times don't get discouraged, It's tough to get used to and this is the first necessary trick for any and every Skateboarder
